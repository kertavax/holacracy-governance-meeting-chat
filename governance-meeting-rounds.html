    To be included in this round are the 6 subrounds of Integrative Decision-Making …

    1. Present Proposal (proposer only)
    2. Clarifying Questions (other participants ask proposer one by one, proposer answers each)
    3. Reaction Round (everyone but proposer speaks)
    4. Amend and Clarify (proposer only)
    5. Objection Round (everyone – including proposer – one at a time). Facilitator-program validates each objection with its owner.
    6. Integration (discussion usually between objector and proposer, others can help)
